## 1. Overview of Cinder

Cinder is a free, open-source, non-custodial desktop application for the Solana blockchain. The Wallet allows you to:

- Generate and manage Solana keypairs and wallet addresses
- Send, receive, and store SOL and SPL tokens
- Swap tokens through third-party decentralized exchange aggregators (currently Jupiter)
- Stake SOL with network validators
- Connect hardware wallets (Trezor, Ledger, Lattice1)
- Interact with AI agents via the Model Context Protocol (MCP)
- View transaction history, token balances, and network data

Cinder is software that runs entirely on your local machine. We do not operate a server that holds your funds, keys, or personal data.
